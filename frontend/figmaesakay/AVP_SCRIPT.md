## 🎨 Presentation Tips

### Voice & Pacing
- **Speak clearly** at a moderate pace (120-150 words/minute)
- **Pause** after introducing each screen (2-3 seconds)
- **Emphasize** key features with vocal inflection
- **Smile** while speaking (it affects your tone)

### Cursor Movement
- **Smooth transitions** between elements
- **Hover briefly** (1-2 seconds) before clicking
- **Circle important items** with slow cursor movement
- **Avoid erratic** or jumpy mouse movements

### Timing
- **Don't rush** transitions - let animations complete
- **Allow comprehension** time (3-5 seconds on key screens)
- **Match narration** to on-screen actions
- **Use silence** strategically for emphasis

### Visual Aids
- **Highlight** important elements with cursor
- **Scroll smoothly** to show more content
- **Zoom in** on critical details (if possible)
- **Return to center** before major transitions
